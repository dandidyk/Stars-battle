# Stars Battle

A fast-paced real-time strategy game for mobile browsers — inspired by Galcon and Auralux.

**[▶ Play Now](https://dandidyk.github.io/Stars-battle/)**

## How to Play

- Stars generate units over time — bigger stars grow faster
- **Select** one of your stars (blue) by tapping or clicking it
- **Attack** by tapping an enemy or neutral star, or **drag** directly from your star to the target
- Send **50% of your troops** per attack — choose your battles wisely
- Capture **all stars** to win

## Controls

| Action | Touch | Mouse |
|---|---|---|
| Select your star | Tap | Click |
| Attack (tap-tap) | Select → tap target | Select → click target |
| Attack (drag) | Hold & drag to target | Hold & drag to target |
| Deselect | Tap empty space | Click empty space |

## Difficulty

| | Stars | AI speed | AI aggression |

| **Easy** | 8 | Slow | Low |
| **Normal** | 12 | Medium | Balanced |
| **Hard** | 16 | Fast | High |

## Stack

Pure **HTML5 Canvas** — zero dependencies, no build step, single file.  
Works on any modern mobile or desktop browser.
